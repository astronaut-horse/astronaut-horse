The Project...

Astronaut horse is a series of open-ended collaborations between visual artists and a group of professional software engineers and machine learning researchers who began working with Stable Diffusion together at the Recurse Center in late 2022.

Stable Diffusion...

Stable Diffusion is a free and open-source alternative to image-generating AI tools like DALL-E and Midjourney which remain private and for-profit. The tools developed for this project are similarly intended to be free for artists to use and help develop. You can read more about it on wikipedia.

Astronaut Horse...

The name astronaut horse refers to the common AI-generated image of an astronaut riding a horse that often acts as a benchmark for the capabilities of generative image models.

AI Workshops...

The project has several student / community workshops planned focused on developing AI literacy through hands-on creative projects. These will introduce participants to basic coding skills and the conceptual foundations of machine learning.

The resources for these workshops will be made freely available as they are developed. The schedule for the currently planned workshops can be found below.
