The image sets below are selections from a series of collaborations with visual artists that began in 2022 with Stable Diffusion fine-tunings (using textual inversion) trained on artist-chosen images. All these trainings can be freely prompted on the live application linked above. Training input images can be viewed through linked documentation, more images are available on discord.
