Open Call to Artists

In late 2022, Stable Diffusion was released to the public. This is a powerful, free and open-source text-to-image AI model that acts as an alternative to proprietary tools like DALL-E and Midjourney.

As a visual artist, I'm not sure how I feel about AI yet. But shortly after Stable Diffusion's release I was given an exciting opportunity to study it and learn to build software tools with it under the guidance of an inspiring group of software engineers and researchers at the Recurse Center.

This work quickly turned into an ongoing series of collaborations with other artists training Stable Diffusion on images of their work and producing thousands of output images in return.

Last month the project was unexpectedly given a hardware grant from a leading AI research and development group. This means a wider range of tools that are easier to use and more artists!
