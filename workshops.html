AI Literacy Artist Workshops

All the AI / machine learning tools used to make the images produced in the collaborations and exhibitions featured on this site are freely linked below and can be used on any computer.

These free tools form the basis for a series of corresponding artist and student workshops but can be used with or without in-person guidance. The workshop notebooks linked below are works in progress and are largely inspired by the free fast.ai course, Hugging Face machine learning resources and the Stable Diffusion research / development community.

If you are interested in hosting a classroom or gallery workshop please reach out via email.
